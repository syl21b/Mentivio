The Beginning

It all started with a simple observation: mental health support should be accessible, immediate, and stigma-free. Inspired by personal experiences and conversations with friends, we began exploring how technology could bridge the gap between struggling and seeking help.

▼

Building With Purpose

I began collaborating with mental health professionals to create tools that bridge the gap between clinical care and everyday struggles. Our goal was simple: make mental health support accessible to everyone, regardless of their circumstances.

August 2025

First Prototype

Developed initial assessment tools and crisis support features, focusing on user privacy and accessibility.

October 2025

Professional Validation

Collaborated with mental health professionals to validate assessment methodologies and ensure ethical implementation.

November 2025

Beta Launch

Released first beta version to a small group of testers, gathering feedback for improvements.

▼

Growing Together

Today, Mentivio continues to evolve through community feedback and professional partnerships. We're committed to creating a safe space where people can understand their mental health, find immediate support during crises, and access resources that empower their journey toward wellbeing.
