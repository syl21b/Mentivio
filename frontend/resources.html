Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need professional help?
If your symptoms are causing significant distress, impacting your daily functioning, or if you're having thoughts of harming yourself or others, it's important to seek professional help.
What's the difference between a psychiatrist and a psychologist?
Psychiatrists are medical doctors who can prescribe medication, while psychologists focus on psychotherapy and psychological testing. Both can provide therapy.
How long does therapy typically take?
The duration varies depending on individual needs, the type of therapy, and the issues being addressed. Some people benefit from short-term therapy (8-12 sessions), while others may engage in longer-term treatment.
Are online therapy services effective?
Research shows that online therapy can be as effective as in-person therapy for many conditions, particularly when provided by licensed professionals using evidence-based approaches.
