If you're in crisis or experiencing suicidal thoughts: Please call or text 988 (U.S.) for free, confidential 24/7 support, or text HOME to 741741. You can also visit 988lifeline.org.

For immediate danger or life-threatening emergencies, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

This assessment is not a substitute for professional or emergency care.
